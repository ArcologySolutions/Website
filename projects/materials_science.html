Materials Science

Innovating Sustainable & Self-Sufficient Building Materials

Mission

To develop and implement sustainable materials that enhance durability, efficiency, and ecological balance in construction and technology.

Key Research Areas

  • Self-healing concrete and bio-composites
  • Recyclable and modular building components
  • High-durability materials for extreme environments
  • Closed-loop waste-to-material conversion
  • Lightweight, high-strength aerogels and composites

Current Projects

  • Developing hempcrete for resilient off-grid structures
  • Testing solar-reactive coatings for temperature regulation
  • Exploring lunar and Martian regolith for extraterrestrial habitats

How to Get Involved

We are seeking engineers, material scientists, and experimental builders. Contact us to collaborate on cutting-edge material research.
