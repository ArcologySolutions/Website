Space Survival

Developing technologies for human sustainability beyond Earth.

Mission

Arcology Solutions aims to create sustainable living solutions for extraterrestrial environments, integrating Earth-based resilience strategies.

Key Challenges

  • Radiation shielding and habitat integrity.
  • Closed-loop resource management (water, air, food).
  • Energy generation and sustainable power sources.
  • Human adaptability and psychological resilience.

Current Research & Projects

  • Prototype closed-loop hydroponic farming for Mars habitats.
  • Testing regolith-based construction materials.
  • Low-energy water extraction technologies for lunar environments.
