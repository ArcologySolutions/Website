Arc-o-munity: Building Together

“Empowering determined hearts and willing hands to build together.”

What is Arc-o-munity?

Arc-o-munity is a collaborative housing initiative designed to bring people together to build sustainable, community-driven living spaces.

Features

  • Designed for cooperative, sustainable living
  • Emphasizes shared spaces & collective responsibility
  • Supports human relations & community governance
  • Customizable for different community needs
  • Integrated with Arc-i-tech and ArcMarket

Who is it for?

Arc-o-munity is ideal for co-housing groups, eco-villages, intentional communities, and anyone seeking sustainable, collective living.
