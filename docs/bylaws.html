Arcology Bylaws

Guiding Principles & Governance Structure

Article I: Name and Purpose

The organization shall be known as ArcologyX, prioritizing sustainable innovation for human and environmental resilience.

Article II: Membership

  • Membership by invitation; skills-based contributions.
  • Categories: Members, Partners, and Friends.
  • Expulsion and banishment policies ensure ethical participation.

Article III: Governance Structure

Decision-making is distributed based on impact and involvement, avoiding a traditional board.

Article IV: Project Committees

Each project operates autonomously with decision-making power among its contributors.

Article V: Meetings

Regular weekly meetings for discussions, with affected parties notified directly.

Article VI: Amendments

Bylaws can be modified by vote among impacted members.

Article VII: Dissolution

Assets will be transferred to a similar nonprofit if the organization dissolves.
