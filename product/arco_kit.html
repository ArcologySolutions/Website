What is Arco-Kit?

Arco-Kit is a modular housing solution designed for individuals and communities looking to build eco-friendly, sustainable homes. It includes all necessary components to construct a single-unit family home using easily assembled modules.

Features

  • Customizable, easy-to-assemble modules
  • Designed for both urban and rural environments
  • Energy-efficient and sustainable materials
  • Adaptable to various climates and extreme conditions
  • Scalable for individual or community projects

Who is it for?

Arco-Kit is designed for sustainable living enthusiasts, off-grid pioneers, community-driven housing initiatives, and anyone looking for an adaptable, future-proof housing solution.
