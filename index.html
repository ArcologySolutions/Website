Arcology Solutions

Arcology Solutions is a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing innovative, adaptable construction solutions for sustainable living markets, agriculture, and extreme climates. Our mission is to design, develop, and distribute adaptive building technologies that empower communities and individuals to thrive in a variety of climates, all while promoting long-term environmental sustainability.

With a strong foundation in modular construction, ecological partnerships, and research-based innovation, Arcology Solutions seeks to revolutionize the way we approach housing and human settlement. Through our comprehensive suite of services, including Arco-Kit modular building kits, Arc-i-tech architecture software, ArcParts marketplace, and Arc-o-munity community housing services, we are shaping the future of sustainable living and community collaboration.

Organization Overview

Arcology Solutions is committed to developing and distributing modular construction kits, consulting services, and an online marketplace for self-sufficient, environmentally conscious living. As climate change and extreme weather conditions become more prevalent, Arcology Solutions focuses on providing adaptive and sustainable building solutions, not just for today, but also for the future.

We work in partnership with leading organizations such as Eco Islands, which specializes in bioremediation and natural waste treatment, and Mars Desert Research Station, which simulates extraterrestrial conditions for research into extreme living environments. Our work is positioned at the intersection of technology, sustainability, and human resilience in extreme conditions.

Core Products and Services

  • The Kit / Arco-Kit / ArkHöm: Modular Housing Kits for Sustainable Living
    Tagline: "Welcome Home... ArkHöm." "Arco-Kit. If you got grit, you can build it."
    The Kit is a modular housing solution designed for individuals and communities looking to build eco-friendly, sustainable homes. The kit includes all necessary components to construct a single-unit family home using easily assembled modules. The kit is designed to be versatile, allowing for customization to meet the unique needs of the end-users, whether in urban or rural areas.
  • Arc-i-tech: Architecture Software and Consultation
    Tagline: "Build your independence today."
    Arc-i-tech is a proprietary architecture software tool that empowers users to design their own sustainable living modules. It allows for precise planning and execution, enabling users to build custom, environmentally friendly housing solutions. This software is offered for free to the public, encouraging user engagement and education in sustainable construction practices. The Arc-i-tech consultation services offer professional expertise to ensure that each project adheres to the highest standards of sustainability and safety.
  • ArcMarket / ArcMall / ArcParts: Online Marketplace for Modular Construction
    Tagline: "Design. Build. Repair. The future is up to you."
    ArcMarket is a digital marketplace where users can purchase parts, tools, and modules related to Arcology Solutions' projects. From replacement components for existing builds to entirely new kits, ArcMarket serves as a one-stop shop for both individuals and small manufacturers. With easy-to-use search filters and the guidance of the marketplace mascot "Stu," users can navigate the platform to find the exact parts they need for their projects.
  • Arc-o-munity: Community Housing and Collaboration
    Tagline: "Empowering determined hearts and willing hands to build together."
    Arc-o-munity focuses on providing sustainable, community-based housing and collaborative living solutions. These arcologies are designed for shared spaces and communal living, which emphasize cooperation, collective responsibility, and social interaction. Through Arc-o-munity, we offer design services and human relations support tailored to the unique needs of community dwellings. These services ensure that community spaces remain harmonious, productive, and sustainable.

Market Opportunity and Impact

Arcology Solutions addresses an emerging global market need for sustainable living solutions that adapt to the challenges posed by climate change and urbanization. Our products and services cater to a broad spectrum of customers, including:

  • Individuals and Families: Seeking affordable, sustainable homes that can withstand extreme environmental conditions.
  • Research Groups and Academic Institutions: In need of innovative, resilient housing and research facilities that simulate extreme conditions or sustainable practices.
  • Nonprofit and Government Organizations: Looking to provide affordable, environmentally responsible housing solutions for displaced populations or communities in need.
  • Manufacturers and Builders: Interested in sourcing sustainable parts and learning from the design expertise of Arcology Solutions.

Financial Strategy and Funding Needs

To successfully launch and scale our operations, Arcology Solutions requires initial funding to:

  • Develop and distribute modular housing kits.
  • Expand the Arc-i-tech platform to support more users and improve the software’s capabilities.
  • Scale the Arcology marketplace to support growing demand.
  • Fund our research and collaboration efforts.
  • Promote public education and outreach efforts about sustainable living solutions.
